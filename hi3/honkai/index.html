Story Timeline

Honkai Impact 3rd's Timeline

Part 1: The Beginning

Schicksal and the Valkyries

The world is plagued by the Honkai, a mysterious force that corrupts humans, creates monsters, and manifests as Herrschers — beings of immense destructive power. To combat this, the organization Schicksal trains female warriors called Valkyries.

  • Kiana Kaslana, a reckless yet determined Valkyrie-in-training, is introduced alongside her classmates Mei Raiden and Bronya Zaychik.
  • They train under Himeko Murata, a veteran Valkyrie, and soon realize that the Honkai threat is far greater than what they imagined.
Part 2

Awakening of the Herrschers

Dark truths begin to unfold as each girl confronts her destiny.

  • Mei becomes the Herrscher of Thunder, losing control of herself before being stopped by Kiana.
  • Bronya struggles with her cybernetic enhancements and loyalty to her homeland, Anti-Entropy.
  • Kiana learns she is not who she thought — she carries the genes of Kiana Kaslana but the body of Kiana clone “K-423”, created by Otto Apocalypse, the Overseer of Schicksal.
  • The Herrscher of the Void (HoV) awakens inside Kiana, unleashing devastation and setting the stage for tragedy.
Part 3

Himeko’s Final Lesson

As the Herrscher of the Void grows stronger, Kiana loses control.

  • Himeko confronts her in a desperate battle, knowing it will cost her life.
  • In her final act, Himeko sacrifices herself to save her students, leaving Kiana with the will to keep fighting.
  • This moment becomes the emotional core of Kiana’s journey, pushing her from a carefree student into a determined warrior.
Part 4

Separation and Struggle

The team scatters as they pursue different paths.

  • Mei, burdened by her Herrscher identity and her desire to protect Kiana, eventually joins World Serpent, the secret organization led by Kevin Kaslana, who seeks to preserve humanity by any means.
  • Kiana isolates herself, wracked by guilt and fear of hurting others, as the Herrscher of the Void continues to consume her.
  • Bronya dives deeper into Anti-Entropy’s struggles, guided by her mentor Welt and the legacy of the Herrscher of Reason.
Part 5

Rebirth and Hope

Through hardship and self-discovery, Kiana learns to accept her Herrscher power without succumbing to it.

  • With the guidance of Fu Hua, Bronya and the memory of Himeko, Kiana awakens as the Herrscher of Flamescion.
  • This rebirth marks her transition into humanity’s beacon of hope, wielding flames that burn away despair.
  • Meanwhile, Fu Hua battles her own inner conflict with the Herrscher of Sentience, eventually regaining her true self.
Part 6

The Elysian Realm

Mei enters the digital world of the Elysian Realm, where the souls of the Flame-Chasers — warriors of the Previous Era — remain.

  • She faces trials against figures like Kalpas, Aponia, the Herrscher of Corruption, and finally Elysia herself.
  • Through these encounters, Mei gains strength and clarity, but also inherits the will of the Flame-Chasers, carrying their ideals into the present.
Part 7

Otto’s Gambit

Otto Apocalypse enacts his final plan: to use the power of the Imaginary Tree to resurrect Kallen, the woman he loved.

  • To achieve this, he manipulates events and even aids the Valkyries at times, leading to the great conflict at Schicksal HQ.
  • Kiana and her sister - Durandal (Real Kiana) confront Otto in a climactic battle. In the end, Otto accepts his fate, sacrifices himself, and entrusts the future to Kiana.
Part 8

The Final Battle Against Kevin

With Otto gone, Kevin becomes the ultimate obstacle.

  • His vision is a world reshaped by stigmata, where humanity can survive at the cost of individuality and freedom.
  • Kiana, Mei, Bronya, and their reunited allies stand against him, determined to protect humanity’s right to choose its future.
  • In an epic showdown, Kevin is defeated, and Mei finally returns to Kiana’s side.
Part 9

The final battle against Honkai

As the ultimate Honkai entity, the Herrscher of the End, awakens to destroy the world, the Valkyries unite for their last stand.

  • Kiana transcends into the Herrscher of Finality, embracing her destiny as humanity’s protector.
  • Together with Mei - Herrscher of Origin, Bronya - Herrscher of Truth, and their companions, she fights the End to preserve the world and the bonds they’ve built.
  • The saga concludes with hope: the Valkyries, once scattered and broken, now stand as one against the future.
