Welcome to my favorite page!

My favorite picture →

Is it a bird? Is it a plane? NO! It's Superman made by modeling clay! As a Picasso's artworks enthusiast, I've always wanted to make an artwork inspired by Picasso, leading to a clay artwork of my favorite super hero - Superman. This picture was taken right before I threw away the box of modeling clay so it is literally the SINGULARLY capture of this masterpiece. Funfact: It used to be my facebook avatar for a long time, and I still use it on some other webs (Scratch, etc)

My favorite anime →

As an anime enthusiast, in my history of over 200 anime, I have never seen a work as strange or extraordinary as this. Heavenly Delusion is captivating because of the atmosphere and setting presented in a slow, mysterious way, with two contrasting storylines eventually converging. Furthermore, the graphics and animation of this show are truly spectacular (IG the GOAT). If you, like me, are really into deep, metaphorical stories, this show is for you.

My favorite manga →

Have you ever spent an entire night reading manga, or cried your eyes out over a character's defeat, or loved a book so much you took a photo album? Medalist made me do all of them. All the characters are charming, the tournaments are exciting and the content is educational, really made me into watching ice skating (OP song is hands down the best one that season).

My favorite music →

It's probably a little hard to pick just one song to describe my favorite music, but if I had to, I would like to discuss Japanese music, which is the type I listen to the most. I'm a huge fan of anime music, especially the songs from Love Live, Revue Starlight and The Apothecary's Diary. Moreover, since her every song is a musical feast for my ears, I adore Aina The End and would not mind listening to her music every day.

My favorite game →

Playing Honkai Impact 3rd for five years has been a significant part of my life. A game that started the 2D anime action game industry, it was a big hit unlike the top hits: Pokémon Go, Dark Souls III, The Legend of Zelda, Overwatch, etc. HI3rd has a devoted following despite losing its former form thanks to its captivating narrative, fully realized characters, and extensive lore set, which no other game can match at this time. To be able to fully express my passion for HI3rd, I would probably have to code a new website.
